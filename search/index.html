How to search

  1. Enter the required word in the search bar.
  2. Select the required list from the drop down.
  3. Click on Fetch Results.
The results are itemized. Click on the induvidual items to expand. Links are present to other parts of the dataset. Click on to search Google for the returned text. External resource links are also present and marked with . Tooltips are available throughout. Best of luck surfing.

  2. "LAK" in "Title"
  3. "University" in "Organization"
